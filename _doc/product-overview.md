# Happy Meal — AI Meal Planner

## Product Overview
Happy Meal is an AI-powered meal planning platform that creates personalized meal plans tailored to each user's health goals, dietary preferences, allergies, and available ingredients. The platform makes healthy eating achievable and sustainable by removing the complexity of nutrition planning.

## Use Context
Happy Meal is a personal-use app — built for individual users managing their own nutrition, not teams or enterprise. The core interaction is one person, their goals, and an AI that knows them well enough to suggest what to eat next.

## Core Problem
Healthy eating is hard to sustain. The most common pain point: **running out of meal ideas** — not knowing what to cook that's healthy, fits dietary restrictions, and uses what's already on hand. Beyond that: tracking nutrition without obsessing over it, shopping efficiently without waste, and sticking to plans that feel generic and disconnected from actual lives. One-size-fits-all plans don't account for individual constraints and preferences, leading to poor adherence and wasted effort.

## Solution
Happy Meal uses AI to generate fully personalized meal plans that adapt over time. The platform learns from user feedback and eating habits to continuously improve its recommendations, turning a complex weekly task into a two-minute review-and-go experience.

## Target Users
- Health-conscious individuals managing specific dietary goals (weight loss, muscle gain, balanced nutrition)
- People with food allergies or dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, keto, Mediterranean, etc.)
- Busy professionals who want to eat well without spending hours planning
- Fitness enthusiasts tracking macros and micronutrients

## Core Features

### 1. Personalized Meal Plan Generation
- Accepts user health goals (weight loss, muscle gain, balanced diet, etc.)
- Filters by dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, keto, Mediterranean, etc.)
- Accounts for food allergies and intolerances
- Generates weekly and daily meal plans from available pantry ingredients

### 2. AI Meal Recommendations
- AI engine recommends meals based on each user's complete profile
- Considers nutritional balance, variety, and personal taste preferences
- Adapts over time based on user feedback and eating patterns

### 3. Smart Shopping Lists
- Auto-generates shopping lists from selected meal plans
- Organizes items by category (produce, dairy, proteins, pantry staples)
- Accounts for ingredients already on hand to avoid redundant purchases

### 4. Nutrition Tracking
- Tracks calories, macros (protein, carbs, fat), and key micronutrients
- Provides daily and weekly nutritional summaries
- Highlights nutritional gaps and achievements against user goals

### 5. Adaptive Recommendations
- Learns from user feedback on each meal (liked / disliked / skipped)
- Adjusts future plans based on eating habits and plan completion rates
- Continuously improves personalization the more users engage

## Strategic Principles
- **Personalization over generics** — every recommendation is tailored to the individual
- **Simplicity first** — the AI handles complexity so users don't have to think about it
- **Habit formation** — the platform improves as users engage more, rewarding consistency
- **Whole-health approach** — balancing taste, nutrition, practicality, and budget
