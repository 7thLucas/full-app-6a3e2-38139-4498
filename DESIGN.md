# Happy Meal Design Guidelines

## Personality
Fresh, warm, appetizing. Clean and modern — not clinical or diet-culture.

## Colors
Primary: warm orange (#F97316)
Accent: fresh green (#22C55E)
Background: soft cream/white (#FFFBF5)
Text: dark warm gray (#1C1917)

## Typography
Headings: Plus Jakarta Sans — friendly and modern
Body: Nunito — approachable and readable

## Components
- Meal cards with vibrant food imagery placeholders
- Progress rings for nutrition tracking
- Simple ingredient chips
- Weekly plan grid view
- Smooth onboarding flow for dietary preferences

## Feel
Every screen should feel like opening a cookbook you actually want to use.
