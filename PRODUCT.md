# Happy Meal

A personal AI meal planning app that eliminates meal idea fatigue. The user is building this for personal use.

## Problem
Running out of meal ideas day-to-day — not knowing what healthy meals to cook.

## Target User
Personal use — an individual who wants to eat healthier but struggles with daily meal decisions.

## Core Feature (MVP)
AI meal plan generator: suggests personalized meals based on health goals, dietary preferences, allergies, and available ingredients. Generates shopping lists, tracks nutrition, and improves recommendations based on user feedback and eating habits.

## App Name
Happy Meal

## Tone
Warm, friendly, encouraging. Like a knowledgeable friend who loves food and health.
